About

 Hi! We're two high schoolers from Montgomery Blair High School in Montgomery County, MD. This snow day predictor is inspired by our friend, Billy!
 We created logistic regression models to predict the chance of a school closure and a 2 hour delay. We used historical weather data (November 2015-April 2019) from Weather Underground and the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration.

The predictors for closure and delays are plotted. The area under the ROC curve, a measure of model accuracy, for the closure model is 0.883 and is 0.9254 for the delay model.
